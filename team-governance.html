Accountability

Governance Framework

Financial Oversight

All accounts independently reviewed by SSB & Associates, Chartered Accountants. Financial decisions above threshold require approval from a minimum of two trustees. Annual financial statements prepared for the Board of Trustees and made available to donors, funders, and regulatory bodies as required.

Board Meetings

The Board of Trustees meets quarterly to review programme progress, financial performance, and strategic direction. Minutes are recorded and filed.
