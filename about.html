About Us

Who We Are

Neurowise Academy Foundation works to ensure that neurodivergent learners in India are recognised, understood, and supported.

Our Story

A Journey of Purpose

Neurowise Academy Foundation was born from a personal journey—one that began in the United Kingdom and gradually found its purpose in India.

Founded by Amita Amlani, the organisation draws from her lived experience of supporting her two neurodivergent children. Navigating assessments, uncertainty, and advocacy led her to retrain as a Specialist Teacher and Assessor for Specific Learning Difficulties (SpLD).

After more than a decade working within the UK’s special educational needs system, her work is shaped by strong UK-based practice and international standards—adapted thoughtfully for India.

After nearly three years engaging with schools across India, clear gaps emerged in awareness, early identification, and access to support. Neurowise Academy Foundation was created in response to these realities.

Today, the foundation works directly with students while equipping teachers, parents, and communities with tools to support neurodivergent learners.

At the heart of our work is a simple belief: every child deserves to be understood.

Mission & Vision

What Drives Us

Our Vision

An education system where every child—regardless of background, learning profile, or neurotype—is recognised, understood, and empowered to thrive.

Our Mission

To strengthen inclusive education in India by improving early identification of learning differences, supporting neurodivergent learners, and building the capacity of educators, families, and communities.
