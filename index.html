About Neurowise Academy Foundation

Neurowise Academy Foundation was formally established in 2026, building on work that began in 2023 through direct engagement with schools, educators, and families across India. Our experience revealed both a growing willingness to understand neurodiversity and significant gaps in early identification, teacher training, and accessible support for children with learning differences. Working at the intersection of technology, social impact, and neuroinclusive education, our work focuses on strengthening early identification, supporting students directly, and equipping educators and communities with evidence-informed tools—helping create learning environments where every child can be recognised, understood, and supported.

About Us

Understanding Every Child

Neurowise Academy Foundation was created in response to a simple but urgent reality—too many children with learning differences are misunderstood or overlooked within the education system.

Our work is shaped by direct experience with students, teachers, and families, and by a deep belief that every child deserves to be recognised for who they are, not defined by what they struggle with.

We exist to help build understanding, confidence, and inclusive learning environments where every child has the opportunity to thrive.

Why Our Work Matters

Closing the Gap

Across India, many children with learning differences are overlooked or misunderstood—often labelled as ‘slow’ or ‘careless.’

Most schools do not yet have access to early screening, trained specialists, or inclusive teaching practices, leaving students without the support they need to thrive.

Neurowise Academy Foundation works to close this gap by building awareness, equipping educators, and supporting children directly—creating pathways for every learner to be recognised, understood, and included.
