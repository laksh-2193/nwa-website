Ways to Get Involved

01 — Join the team

Work With Us

We are always looking for passionate individuals who share our commitment to inclusive education. Whether you have experience in education, psychology, social work, or nonprofit management, there may be a role for you.

Current and future opportunities will be listed here. If you are interested in joining our team, please reach out directly.

Get in touch
02 — Give your time

Volunteers & Internships

Volunteers play an important role in our work. Whether you can offer a few hours a week or commit to a longer placement, your time and skills can make a real difference.

We welcome volunteers and interns from education, psychology, communications, data analysis, and related fields. International volunteers are also welcome.

Apply to volunteer
03 — Build together

Partnerships

We partner with schools, universities, corporates, and other organisations to expand our reach and deepen our impact. If your organisation is committed to inclusive education or CSR in education, we would love to explore how we can work together.

Current partners include Wipro Foundation, Makkala Jagriti, and Azim Premji Foundation.

Discuss a partnership
04 — Fund the work

Donate

Your financial support enables early identification, student support, teacher training, and long-term change in schools across India. All donations are eligible for 80G tax exemption.
